# Jam Session Guide

This site is a shared song notebook for the jam.

It helps everyone see who is around, what songs people know, and who plays or sings what. No big setup, no pressure, no perfect catalog needed. Just add your stuff and look around.

Open it here: https://dnomads-jam-fln.duckdns.org

## Add Yourself

1. Click **Create profile**.
2. Enter your email and choose a password.
3. Write your name.
4. Search for a song you like to play or sing.
5. Click the song when it shows up.
6. Choose what you play or sing on that song.
7. Click **+ Add Song**.
8. Add another song if you feel like it.
9. Click **Save**.

Done. You are on the list.

## Add a Different Instrument

If your instrument is not there, choose **Other**.

Type the instrument name, then click **+ Add Instrument**.

That can be anything: cajon, trumpet, flute, spoons, a tiny synth, your mysterious noise box, whatever.

## See Who Is Playing

Open **The Crew** to see everyone in the jam.

Each card shows a person, what they play, and some songs they added.

Click a person to see their full song list.

Want to find all guitar players, singers, drummers, bass players, or keys people? Use the instrument buttons near the top.

## Find Something Fast

Use the search box when you are looking for someone or something.

You can search for:

- a person
- a song
- a band
- an artist

For example, search **Beatles** to see Beatles songs, or search a friend's name to find their songs.

## Check the Songs

Open **Songbook** to see the songs people have added.

Click a song to see who has it and what they play or sing on it.

This is the place to check when someone says, "Hey, can we play this?"

## Browse by Band

Open **Bandbook** if you want to browse by artist or band.

Click a band, then click a song to see who has that song.

Songs without a clear artist may show up under **Originals / Unknown**.

## Change Your Profile

Need to add a song, remove one, or fix something?

1. Log in with the same email and password you used when you added yourself.
2. Find yourself in **The Crew**.
3. Click **Edit**.
4. Change whatever you need.
5. Click **Save**.

Only you and the admin can edit or remove your profile.

## Remove Someone

If a profile was added by mistake, or someone wants it removed:

1. Log in with the same email used for that profile, or ask the admin.
2. Find the person in **The Crew**.
3. Click the trash button.
4. Confirm it.

That is it. Add a few songs, find people to play with, and make some noise.
